Price Match Paradise is a barcode-scanning grocery app that compares live prices across 50+ stores including Walmart, Target, Kroger, Costco, and Amazon Fresh. Users scan a barcode in the aisle, see the cheapest option nearby, and show their screen at checkout to get a price match — all in under 2 seconds. Built with Swift/SwiftUI for iOS and Kotlin for Android, with a Firebase backend powering real-time price data.

Real-time barcode scanner — 2-second results
Live price comparison across 50+ stores
Smart receipt scanner — finds what you overpaid
Price-drop alerts for items you buy regularly
Monthly savings dashboard & history
Privacy-first — data never sold or shared
